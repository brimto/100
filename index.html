Sightseeings of USA.

Monument Valley (Arizona) Interesting monuments of nature and culture of the United States - historical buildings, national parks, unique natural formations - concentrated in Arizona. A separate point in the journey to these lands deserves an unusual geological monument - Monument Valley in the north-east of the state. This is the land of amazing sandstone cliffs, the height of which reaches 300 meters. Hundreds of millions of years ago, on the site of a deserted plain where these rocks rise, was the sea. Silt settled at its bottom for many millennia, compressing sand and forming porous sandstone, which in turn turned into slate. Later, the layer of the earth's crust rose strongly, and the sea went away, and in its place a huge sandstone plateau formed. Under the influence of water and wind, the plateau was eroded, and in our time only the strongest rocky columns, or Monuments, have remained of it.

National Alley, Washington National Park (National Mall) is designed as a cross, it is here that the White House and the Thomas Jefferson Memorial, the Abraham Lincoln Memorial and the Capitol are located. The Washington Monument rises in the center of the National Mall. You can slowly walk between the majestic buildings, visit the Botanical Gardens or the Smithsonian Institution Museum Complex (Holocaust Museum, Natural History, Aerospace, American Indian Museum, National Gallery of Art or Museum of Journalism and News).
